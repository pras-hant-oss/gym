About Us

WE ARE TITAN FITNESS

Founded in 2009, Titan Fitness Gym has established itself as the premier destination for serious fitness enthusiasts in Kathmandu. What started as a small training facility has evolved into a comprehensive fitness empire, serving over 5,000 members with world-class equipment and unparalleled coaching.

Our mission is simple: to transform lives through fitness, community, and unwavering support. We believe that everyone has the potential to achieve greatness, and our role is to provide the environment, tools, and guidance to make that happen.

FAQ

COMMON QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know before joining Titan Fitness.

What are the gym operating hours?

We are open 7 days a week from 5:00 AM to 10:00 PM. On public holidays, we operate from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Our facilities are fully staffed during all operating hours for your safety and convenience.

Do you offer personal training services?

Yes! We have certified personal trainers available for one-on-one sessions. Packages start from Rs. 15,000/month for 12 sessions. Each session is 60 minutes and includes a customized workout plan, nutrition guidance, and progress tracking.

What types of memberships do you offer?

We offer Monthly (Rs. 3,500), Quarterly (Rs. 9,000), Annual (Rs. 30,000), Personal Training (Rs. 15,000/month), and Premium VIP (Rs. 50,000/year) memberships. All memberships include full gym access, with higher tiers including group classes, personal training sessions, and nutrition plans.

Is there a joining fee or registration fee?

We charge a one-time registration fee of Rs. 1,000 which includes your membership card, fitness assessment, and body composition analysis. However, we frequently run promotions where this fee is waived.

Can I freeze or pause my membership?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year for medical reasons or travel. A nominal fee of Rs. 500/month applies during the freeze period. Please notify us at least 7 days in advance.

What equipment brands do you use?

We feature premium equipment from Technogym, Life Fitness, Hammer Strength, Rogue Fitness, and Eleiko. Our cardio zone includes the latest treadmills with entertainment systems, and our strength area has both plate-loaded and selectorized machines.

Do you provide nutrition and diet plans?

Absolutely. Our certified nutritionists create customized meal plans based on your goals, dietary preferences, and lifestyle. Nutrition counseling is included in Annual, Personal Training, and VIP memberships. Individual consultations are also available for Rs. 2,000/session.

Are there group fitness classes available?

Yes! We offer Yoga, Zumba, HIIT, CrossFit, Strength Training, and Functional Fitness classes. Classes are included in Quarterly, Annual, and VIP memberships. Drop-in class rates are Rs. 500 per session for non-members.

What is the minimum age requirement to join?

Members must be at least 16 years old. Those aged 16-17 require parental consent and supervision during their first month. We also offer a dedicated Teen Fitness program for ages 13-15 on weekends.

Do you have locker rooms and showers?

Yes, we have separate locker rooms for men and women with digital lockers, rain showers, vanity stations, and premium toiletries. We also offer a steam room and dry sauna for recovery.

Is parking available at the gym?

Yes, we have a dedicated parking area with space for 50+ vehicles. Parking is free for all members during their workout sessions. We also have secure bicycle parking.

Can I bring a guest or friend?

Annual and VIP members receive 4 guest passes per month. Additional guests can visit for Rs. 1,000 per day. All guests must sign a waiver and attend an orientation before using equipment.

What safety measures do you have in place?

We maintain hospital-grade sanitation with 3x daily deep cleaning. All staff are CPR and First Aid certified. We have AED devices on every floor, 24/7 CCTV surveillance, and trained security personnel.

Do you offer corporate or group discounts?

Yes! We offer corporate wellness packages for companies with 10+ employees. Group discounts of 15-25% are available for families and friends signing up together. Contact our sales team for custom quotes.

What should I bring for my first visit?

Bring a valid ID, comfortable workout clothes, athletic shoes, a water bottle, and a small towel. We provide lockers, but you may bring your own lock. We also sell gym essentials at our front desk if you forget anything.

How do I book a free trial session?

Simply fill out the Free Trial form on our website or message us on WhatsApp at +977 9768832052. We'll confirm your booking within 2 hours. Your trial includes a full gym tour, fitness assessment, and a 45-minute workout session with a trainer.

Do you have Wi-Fi in the gym?

Yes, we offer high-speed fiber Wi-Fi throughout the facility. The network name is "TitanFitness_Guest" and the password is available at the front desk. Our cardio machines also have built-in entertainment systems with Netflix and YouTube.

What is your cancellation policy?

Monthly memberships can be cancelled with 15 days notice. Quarterly and Annual memberships are non-refundable but can be transferred to another person for a Rs. 1,000 transfer fee. Please review our full terms at the front desk.

Are your trainers certified?

All Titan Fitness trainers hold internationally recognized certifications including NSCA, ACE, ISSA, CrossFit L1-L3, and Yoga Alliance RYT-200/500. They also undergo continuous in-house education and monthly performance reviews.

Do you have a nutrition bar or cafe?

Yes! Our Titan Nutrition Bar serves protein shakes, pre-workout drinks, healthy meals, and snacks. We also offer meal prep services for members. All items are designed by our nutritionists to support your fitness goals.

How crowded does the gym get?

Peak hours are typically 6:00-9:00 AM and 5:00-8:00 PM on weekdays. We limit memberships to ensure equipment is always available. Our facility spans 15,000 sq ft with over 200 pieces of equipment, so even during peak times, wait times are minimal.
